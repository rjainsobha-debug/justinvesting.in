What are Mutual Funds?

A Mutual Fund is a professionally managed investment scheme, usually run by an Asset Management Company that pools money from many investors and invests it in stocks, bonds, and other securities.

"Mutual funds provide a way for individuals to participate in professional, diversified portfolios even with small amounts of capital."

The Power of SIP

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way of investing where you contribute a fixed amount at regular intervals. It leverages rupee-cost averaging and the magical power of compounding.
