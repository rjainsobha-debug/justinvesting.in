Health, Car & Personal Accident

Protection options customers actively ask for.

Health policy types commonly available through InsuranceDekho and Square Insurance include individual, family floater, senior citizen, critical illness, and related protection options. Square Insurance also highlights major car insurance structures like third-party, standalone own-damage, and comprehensive cover. Personal accident cover is also listed as a separate protection need.

Individual Health Insurance

Best when one adult needs a separate sum insured and clean individual risk profile.

Family Floater

Useful for spouse + children under one shared sum insured when ages are relatively close and claims are manageable.

Senior Citizen Plans

Built for parents and older adults with age-related hospitalization risk, waiting periods, and claim usability in focus.

Critical Illness Cover

Useful as a lump-sum financial buffer for specified major illnesses, separate from regular hospitalization cover.

Car Insurance

Choose between mandatory third-party cover, standalone own-damage, or comprehensive cover depending on vehicle use, age, and protection need.

Personal Accident Cover

Adds an extra shield for accidental death, disability, and income disruption risk beyond regular health plans.

What we compare

Sum insured, room rent, waiting period, co-pay, cashless usability.

What we avoid

Wrong floater mix, under-covering parents, low claim-friendly options.
